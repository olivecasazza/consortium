§consortium-slurm

Slurm job orchestration with nix-built environments.

Uses nix to build hermetic job environments, copies them to the submit node, then submits jobs with slurm. Supports arbitrary DAG pipelines (e.g., RNA-seq bioinformatics workflows) via DagBuilder.
