# Free OpenRouter model seed for the accountant ledger

Researched 2026-04-25. Source: `GET https://openrouter.ai/api/v1/models` filtered to
`pricing.prompt == 0 && pricing.completion == 0 && context_length >= 32768`,
cross-referenced against OpenRouter model cards, upstream HF cards, and independent
benchmarks (SWE-bench Verified, BFCL v3, Artificial Analysis).

**OpenRouter free-tier rate limits (apply to every `:free` row below):**
20 req/min; 50 req/day anonymous; **1000 req/day with $10 lifetime credit on file**.
Failed requests count. 429s under load are common on the popular `:free` slugs
(qwen3-coder, gpt-oss-120b) — always wire a fallback.

Excluded from the table: deprecated-this-week (`inclusionai/ling-2.6-flash:free`
sunset 2026-04-29; `inclusionai/ling-2.6-1t:free` sunset 2026-04-30); too small for
autonomous code edits (`liquid/lfm-2.5-1.2b-*`, `meta-llama/llama-3.2-3b`,
`google/gemma-3-4b`); off-task (`google/lyria-3-*` music; `baidu/qianfan-ocr-fast`
OCR; `cognitivecomputations/dolphin-mistral-24b-venice-edition` uncensored variant;
`openrouter/free` router meta-model; `nvidia/nemotron-nano-12b-v2-vl` vision-only).

### Top free picks for code-edit + tool-use (in order)

1. **`minimax-m2.5-free`** — SWE-Bench Verified 80.2%, Multi-SWE-Bench 51.3%, **BFCL multi-turn 76.8** (beats Claude 4.5's 68.0 and Gemini 3 Pro's 61.0). If it stays up, it's the answer.
2. **`qwen3-coder-free`** — purpose-built for repo-level agentic coding; only loses on rate-limit reliability.
3. **`glm-4.5-air-free`** — best tool-call track record in the GLM family; safer bet than gpt-oss-120b when Harmony quirks bite.

### Top free pick for long-context

**`qwen3-coder-free`** at 262k — uniquely trained on repo-level reasoning. Nemotron 3 Super advertises 1M upstream but OR caps it at 262k, same tier — and lacks the code-specific RL.
